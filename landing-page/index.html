My Task

My task feature in Hireme, allows you to keep track of all your recruitment-related tasks, making it easy to stay organized and on top of your recruiting efforts. This feature allows you to create a list of tasks, set deadlines, and assign them to team members.

Custom Email

Custom Email Template feature in Hireme, allows you to personalize your communication with candidates and make your communication stand out. This feature enables you to create custom email templates and use them to communicate with candidates throughout the recruitment process.

Job Template

Job Template feature in Hireme, enables you to create and save job templates, which can be used to quickly create new job postings. This feature saves you time and effort by allowing you to reuse your job templates when you need to post new job openings.

Scheduling

Scheduling feature in Hireme, allows you to schedule interviews, events and appointments with candidates and team members. This feature provides you with a user-friendly calendar and helps you to manage your recruitment-related activities in one place.

Job Overview

Job Overview feature in Hireme, provides you with an overview of all your job postings, including the status of each posting, the number of applicants, and the number of hires.

This feature allows you to easily monitor the progress of your job postings, and make adjustments as needed.

With the Job Overview feature, you can stay on top of your recruiting efforts and ensure that you are reaching the right audience for each job posting.
